## FAQ

**Q: Is this actually free?**
A: Yes. No trial, no credit card, no limit. The audit is free because it's also how Credex finds teams overspending on AI tools — and for high-savings cases, we'll show you how to get the same tools cheaper through Credex's credit marketplace.

**Q: Do I need to log in or create an account?**
A: No. Fill in your stack, get your audit instantly. We only ask for your email if you want us to send you the report — and that's optional.

**Q: How do you know the pricing is accurate?**
A: Every number is sourced directly from the vendor's official pricing page, with the URL and verification date shown in the report. We re-verify all pricing weekly.

**Q: What do you do with my email if I share it?**
A: We send you the audit report and — if your savings opportunity is significant — a note from the Credex team about how to capture more of that savings through discounted credits. No spam. You can unsubscribe in one click.

**Q: What if I'm already spending optimally?**
A: We'll tell you that honestly. "Your stack is well-optimized for your team size and use case." We don't manufacture savings — that would make the tool useless. We'll still offer to notify you when new cost optimizations become available for your stack.
